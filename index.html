Getcha Fix Virtual Bar Trivia

Welcome! Getcha Fix Virtual Bar Trivia will bring the bar to the comfort of your own home! There will be 10 questions from every category across the board (General Knowledge, Entertainment, Math, Science, Anime, Art, Mythology, etc.)
Rules are simple.

  1. Make a drink (Not sure what to make? Get inspired by our Drink of the Moment
    or click on the list of Classic Drinks below)
  2. Sit back and take a hit
  3. Hit the 'Start Bar Trivia' Button
  4. Test your knowledge (TIP: Jam out to some MF DOOM by hitting the Jukebox
    in the top left corner)

Good luck and Have fun!
